Meet Janet

Janet Clifford

"Sharing my knowledge and empowering people with the energy is a good way to awaken that spark. When you feel it, it can be life changing."

Reiki Master Teacher. Sound healer. Shamanic practitioner. Based in Cleckheaton, West Yorkshire. Janet works intuitively, using crystals, tuning forks and the shamanic drum when the energy calls for them.

Her mission is to open people to an awareness of their own energy and the power they hold to harness it. Feeling love and connection. Trusting in something higher. Feeling safe in the world.
