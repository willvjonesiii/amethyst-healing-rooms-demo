The Vision

"We all have the power to heal ourselves — we just need to remember what we already know deep within us."

Amethyst Healing Rooms is a place where everyone comes together through an awareness of the energy that connects us all — and the love that we truly are. A place without judgement, where people feel safe and loved.

The mission is to open people to an awareness of their own energy — and the power they hold to harness it, bringing real change in their perception of the world and of themselves. Feeling love. Feeling connection. Trusting in something higher. Feeling safe in the world.

Meet Your Guide

Janet Clifford

"Sharing my knowledge and empowering people with the energy is a good way to awaken that spark — when you feel it, it can be life changing."

Janet is a Reiki Master Teacher based in Cleckheaton, West Yorkshire, specialising in Usui Reiki, sound healing and shamanic practice. Her approach is deeply intuitive — she uses crystals, tuning forks and the shamanic drum not by formula, but by feel, guided by whatever each person needs in that moment.

Over the years, Janet has built something rare: not just a practice, but a tribe. Many of the people she has trained in Reiki are now practitioners themselves, forming a living community that holds monthly shares, circles and gatherings where the energy keeps flowing.
