The Vision
"We all have the power to heal ourselves. We just need to remember what we already know deep within us."

Amethyst Healing Rooms is a place where everyone comes together through an awareness of the energy that connects us all, and the love that we truly are. No judgement. No conditions. Just safety, warmth and the space to feel.

When you feel the energy, it can be life changing.

Meet Your Guide

Janet Clifford

"Sharing this knowledge and empowering people with the energy is a good way to awaken that spark. When you feel it, it can be life changing."

Janet is a Reiki Master Teacher based in Cleckheaton, West Yorkshire, specialising in Usui Reiki, sound healing and shamanic practice. Her approach is entirely intuitive, guided by whatever each person needs in that moment.

Over the years, she has built not just a practice, but a tribe. A living community of people she has trained, who now practise themselves, who gather monthly and carry the energy forward.
