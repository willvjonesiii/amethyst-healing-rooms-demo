Enquire about training

Your first day with reiki is a special one. It could be likened to an awakening of the spiritual essence we all are. Once you feel the reiki energy for yourself it can be quite profound. Open to it, trust the process, and it takes you on a journey of self-discovery — remembering your true self, and slowly finding that calm space within.

About the day

Groups of no more than four, in a relaxed, calm space. Trust that the universe will align the right people to share the day with you — energetically matched, so you feel at ease.

The morning covers the history of reiki and the founder Mikao Usui: how reiki came to be, the lineage of those who continued his work, and how it became one of the world's most recognised energy healing modalities. You then receive your attunement — opening you to the energy for self-healing and healing others.

The afternoon is practical. You work with the energy through meditations and hands-on healing originally taught by Mikao Usui. The chakra system and its relationship to reiki is explained. The day finishes with you giving and receiving a full-body hands-on reiki session.

You leave with a certificate and a full-colour 42-page manual. Join the community through the WhatsApp group and monthly reiki share meet-ups.

A lovely group of like-minded people are waiting to meet you.

Level 2 introduces three symbols that bring a more focused energy. The first two work on a physical, mental and emotional level. The third brings a feeling of oneness and is used for distant healing — the ability to send healing to any living being, anywhere, just as powerfully as being in the same room.

Level 2 also develops intuitive working: following that inner knowing to more easily direct the energy where it is needed. There are further tools at this level for more powerful, focused healing sessions.

Third degree reiki (Shinpiden) is traditionally regarded as the Master level. It is important to be clear that initiation into third degree reiki does not turn anyone into an enlightened spiritual master — however it is a spiritual contract to undertake to evolve to that stage. At this stage further change is inevitable, and you will be well aware of this from the changes that have already occurred on the way to the Master degree.

About the course

The Master/Teacher level is a two-day course. At this level the Usui Master symbol is taught along with Master self-empowerment meditations and how to give a more focused healing attunement.

As this level entitles you to pass on reiki to others, you will also learn how to perform the reiki energy attunements — the sacred act of opening another person to the energy, just as Janet once opened you.
