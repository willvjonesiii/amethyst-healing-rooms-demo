The Practice

How we work together

Janet's Approach

Every session is intuition-led.

Janet doesn't follow a fixed protocol. She uses crystals, tuning forks and the shamanic drum when the energy calls for them. Pricing is equally intuitive. No rigid price list. Each session is shaped by what that person needs.

01

Reiki Healing

Reiki is a Japanese technique for stress reduction and relaxation that promotes healing. It works with the universal life energy that flows through all living things, channelled through gentle touch.

Janet may incorporate crystals, tuning forks or the shamanic drum into a session depending on what arises. No two sessions are identical. You arrive as you are and leave as something shifts.

"Each session is shaped by what that person needs in that moment."

02

Reiki and Sound Healing

A private 1:1 session that combines the gentle holding power of reiki with therapeutic sound. Tuning forks calibrated to specific frequencies are placed on or near the body. The gong and crystal bowls may also be used.

Sound works on a cellular level, loosening what is held tight. Reiki holds the space for it to release. Together they are deeply restorative. Most people describe leaving re-tuned.

Suitable for those new to both practices and experienced alike.

03

Meditation and Sound Healing

Small group sessions of up to six people. Janet plays crystal singing bowls and gong, creating a soundscape that the body simply surrenders into. No meditation experience is needed. You lie down, close your eyes, and let the sound do the work.

The crystal bowls are each tuned to different frequencies corresponding to the body's energy centres. The gong clears and resets. Most people describe an experience they feel for days afterwards.

Maximum 6 per session. A genuinely intimate experience.

04

Monthly Sound Healing Group

Open monthly community sound healing sessions held at a local gathering space in Cleckheaton. Open to everyone. No previous experience needed and no booking required, just turn up.

The collective experience of sound is different to a private session. There is something that happens when a group of people surrender to the same frequency at the same time. It has its own kind of power.

Check Facebook for upcoming dates and location details.

05

Monthly Shamanic Drumming

The drum is the oldest healing technology we know. Across every ancient culture, the rhythmic beat of the drum was the bridge between ordinary consciousness and the deeper layers of the self.

Janet hosts monthly shamanic drumming circles at a local community space in Cleckheaton. No experience needed. You do not need to understand it before you come. You only need to be willing to be present.

Check Facebook for upcoming dates and location details.

06

Usui Reiki Training

Janet's greatest passion. Teaching Usui Reiki and passing on the gift of energy healing to others is her deepest calling. She offers training at all levels, from complete beginners through to Reiki Master.

Training is much more than learning a technique. Janet describes it as helping people remember something that was already in them. Many of her students have gone on to become practitioners themselves, joining the wider Amethyst community.

Full details on the training page
