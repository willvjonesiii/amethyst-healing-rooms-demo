Who We Are

A lineage of
love and energy.

Over many years, Janet has trained and attuned people to Reiki. Several of them are now practitioners themselves. Together they form something rare in the wellness world: a genuine tribe. Not a brand. Not a directory. A living community of people who felt the energy, had their spark awakened, and chose to carry it forward.

This is exactly what Janet set out to build. A place where everyone comes together through an awareness of the energy that connects us all, and the love that we truly are. No hierarchy. No judgement. Just safety, warmth and the deep knowing that you are not alone in this.

Whether you come as a complete beginner or a seasoned practitioner, you are welcome here. The door is always open.

Monthly Event

The Reiki Share

Once a month, the Amethyst community comes together to share reiki energy. It is simple: everyone gives and everyone receives. Practitioners and newcomers sit in circle together. There is no performance and no pressure. Just people holding space for one another in the most genuine way.

The reiki share is the heartbeat of this community. It is where connections are made, where the energy deepens, and where people who came alone discover they were never really alone at all.

All are welcome, whether you have been attuned to reiki or not. Janet will guide you through what to expect before you arrive.
